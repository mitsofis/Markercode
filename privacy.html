MarkerCode Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2025

1. Introduction

MarkerCode is a Progressive Web App (PWA) developed by mitsof to help developers copy, replace, or remove code between comment markers. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use MarkerCode, whether accessed via https://mitsof.github.io/markercode/ or installed from the Google Play Store.

2. Data We Collect

MarkerCode does not collect, store, or process any personal data. The app uses localStorage in your browser to temporarily store markers (e.g., , // End) you create or input. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

The localStorage data is used solely to enable MarkerCode’s functionality, such as saving your custom markers for use during your session. No other use is made of this data.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties, as no personal or user data is collected by MarkerCode.

5. Third-Party Services

MarkerCode uses the following third-party services, which do not collect personal data:

  • Tailwind CSS (via CDN): For styling.
  • Google Fonts (via CDN): For typography.
  • Toastify JS (via CDN): For notifications.

These services are loaded directly by your browser and do not involve data collection by MarkerCode.

6. Your Rights

Since MarkerCode does not collect personal data, there are no personal data rights to exercise. You can clear localStorage data by:

  • Clearing your browser’s cache and storage.
  • Uninstalling the PWA if installed.

7. Security

As all data is stored locally on your device, MarkerCode does not implement server-side security measures. We recommend keeping your device and browser secure to protect your local data.

8. Children’s Privacy

MarkerCode is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children, and the app’s functionality is intended for professional developers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in MarkerCode’s functionality or legal requirements. Updates will be posted at https://mitsof.github.io/markercode/privacy.html, and the “Last updated” date will be revised.
